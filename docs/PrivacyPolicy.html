Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2025

1. Introduction

SocialLens (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you connect your Facebook or Instagram account and use our analytics features.

2. Data We Collect

  • Profile Data: Name, profile picture, user ID.
  • Posts & Media: Captions, timestamps, comments count, likes count.
  • Engagement Data: Comments text, reactions, follower counts (for Business accounts).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your social data solely to power the analytics dashboards you see in-app: sentiment analysis, engagement metrics, growth trends, etc. We do not share your personal data with third parties except with your explicit permission.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for your session authentication and to remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser, though some features may stop working.

5. Data Security

We store your access tokens encrypted, and all API traffic is over HTTPS. Our servers are hosted on a SOC-2 compliant provider.

6. Your Rights

You may disconnect your social accounts at any time. To delete all your data, contact us at help@sociallens.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy—when we do, we’ll revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance.
